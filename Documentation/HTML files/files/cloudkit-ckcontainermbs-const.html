CKContainerMBS.ErrorParticipantMayNeedVerification = 33
Type Topic Plugin Version
const CloudKit MBS Mac64bit Plugin 16.5
Function: One of the error constants.
Notes: Share Metadata cannot be determined, because the user is not a member of the share. There are invited participants on the share with email addresses or phone numbers not associated with any iCloud account. The user may be able to join the share if they can associate one of those email addresses or phone numbers with their iCloud account via the system Share Accept UI. Call UIApplication's openURL on this share URL to have the user attempt to verify their information.
